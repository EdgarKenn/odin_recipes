Broccoli Cauiliflower Casserole

This broccoli and cauliflower casserole is always a hit! The crushed croutons on top give it that crunchy dimension that everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 package (15 oz) seasoned crounons, crushed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Place broccoli and cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water, and bring to a boil; cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes.

    and arrange in an 11x7-inch baking dish.

  3. Beat eggs in a bowl until creamy. Stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion; pour over vegetables.

    Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbing, about 40 minutes
